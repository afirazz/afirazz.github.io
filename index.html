Hi, I'm Afira!

I'm an Environmental Geoscientist, a Data Enthusiast, and an Aspiring Developer from Boorloo/Perth, Western Australia

About Me

I'm an environmental geoscientist with a passion for solving environmental challenges with the power of data and technology. I started to self-study coding out of a desire to tackle data analysis more efficiently and joined the She Codes Plus program in 2024 to build a strong foundation in software development in a supportive community.

I’m an advocate for diversity and inclusion in STEM and I'm excited to volunteer and give back to the community that empowered me to code.

Outside of work, I love petting my grey tabby cat Joanna, hiking trails with cool geological formations, diving into fantasy and sci-fi books, playing amateur badminton, and enthusiastically gaming — whether it’s video games, board games, or tabletop role-playing adventures.
